Crestonyx Lagoon Resort & Hotel

Experience Waterfront Living at Its Finest

Luxury Accommodation • Distinctive Dining • Leisure • Entertainment

Nestled beside the gentle waters of a scenic lagoon, Crestonyx Lagoon Resort & Hotel offers a welcoming retreat where contemporary style, exceptional service, and natural surroundings come together. Created for guests seeking both relaxation and recreation, the resort provides an inviting atmosphere complemented by elegant accommodations, diverse dining options, and a selection of leisure experiences.

Whether you're travelling for business, celebrating a special occasion, or simply taking time away, Crestonyx delivers a stay defined by comfort, quality, and genuine hospitality.

A Setting Designed to Inspire

The character of the lagoon is reflected throughout the resort, from bright open spaces and modern architecture to carefully designed interiors that emphasize comfort and tranquility. Large windows invite natural light indoors, while thoughtfully landscaped surroundings create a peaceful backdrop for every visit.

Every detail has been considered to ensure a stay that feels effortless from arrival through departure.

Access Requirements

Gaming facilities are available exclusively to guests who are 18 years of age or older, in accordance with local regulations.

  • Valid photo identification may be requested.
  • Entry to gaming areas is prohibited for underage individuals.
  • Resort staff and security teams help maintain a safe and enjoyable environment for all guests.

Responsible Gaming

Gaming should always be enjoyed responsibly as part of a well-balanced leisure experience.

Helpful Guidelines

  • Decide on a personal spending limit before participating.
  • Play for entertainment rather than financial gain.
  • Take breaks regularly.
  • Make time to enjoy the resort's dining and recreational amenities.
  • Avoid gaming while under emotional or financial pressure.
  • Never chase previous losses.

Guest Assistance

Available Support

  • Guidance on voluntary self-exclusion programs
  • Confidential assistance from trained resort personnel

Responsible Gaming Notice

Gaming facilities are intended exclusively for guests who meet the legal minimum age requirement of 18 years. Identification may be requested to verify eligibility before entry.

Guests are encouraged to participate responsibly and to view gaming as one element of a broader leisure experience. Individuals who believe gambling may be affecting their personal wellbeing, finances, employment, or relationships are encouraged to seek assistance from qualified support organizations. Crestonyx Lagoon Resort & Hotel remains dedicated to promoting responsible gaming while providing a welcoming, secure, and enjoyable environment for every guest.
